About the Game

Cards For Cowboys is a deck-building card game set in the rugged frontier. Build your deck, outsmart your rivals, and become the most legendary cowboy in the West.

Build Your Deck

Draft cards from the saloon to assemble a winning hand.

Outplay Your Rivals

Use strategy and bluffing to come out on top.

2-4 Players

Quick games that play in 30-45 minutes.

How to Play

  1. Start with a basic deck of cowboy cards.
  2. Each round, buy new cards from the saloon market.
  3. Combine cards for powerful combos and actions.
  4. The cowboy with the most points at the end wins!
