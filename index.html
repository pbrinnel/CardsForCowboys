About the Game

Cards For Cowboys is a deck-building card game where luck meets strategy in the Wild West. Push your luck by drawing cards, but watch out for Bandits -- draw three and you bust! Spend your coins to buy powerful new cards from the solitaire-style store, grow your herd of Cows, and outscore your rivals across three thrilling Acts.

2-4 Players

Scales with player count using a unique solitaire pyramid store.

Push Your Luck

Draw cards for coins and cows, but three Bandits and you bust!

Build Your Deck

Buy cards from the store to strengthen your deck across 3 Acts.

Risk vs. Reward

Track card backs by cacti count -- 1 is safe, 3 is high risk, high reward.

How to Play

1. Draw Phase

All players draw cards from their personal deck one at a time. Stop whenever you want -- but if you flip three Bandits, you bust and lose your turn!

2. Buy Phase

Spend the coins you drew to buy a card from the solitaire store, adding it to your deck. Or burn a card to reveal what's hidden underneath.

3. Score Cows

If you didn't bust, add any Cows you drew this round to your Herd. The player with the most Cows after all three Acts wins!
